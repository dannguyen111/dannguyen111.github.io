Gettysburg College Fashion Initiative

Creating a space for self-expression and philanthropy through our love for fashion.

My Role: President & Founder

In January 2024, I founded the Gettysburg College Fashion Initiative to empower students to express themselves, leading a 10-member executive board across five distinct departments.

Part of the founding executive board.

Beyond just "fashion," this organization served as a major operational challenge that honed my leadership skills outside of the technical realm. It required managing large budgets, coordinating logistics for hundreds of attendees, and fostering a creative community.

Key Achievements

The Fashion Show
Creative Director

Directed a full-scale fashion show drawing over 300 attendees. I managed a $10,000 budget, overseeing everything from stage design and lighting to model coordination and marketing.

Vintage Market Philanthropy
Fundraising

Established and ran a semiannual Vintage Market on campus. This initiative not only promoted sustainable fashion but successfully raised $9,000 for charity.

Organizational Growth
Leadership

Built the organization from the ground up, structuring an executive board to handle finance, marketing, logistics, and creative direction, ensuring the club's longevity beyond my tenure.
